---
title: "Discord deployment"
description: "Turn a verb into a Discord bot with the right permissions."
---

## What you need

- A Discord application
- A bot token
- The right intents turned on

<Warning>
  Enable all privileged intents in the Discord Developer Portal. Without them,
  the bot will not come online or read messages.
</Warning>

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Discord application">
    Go to the Discord Developer Portal and create a new app. Add a bot user.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the bot token">
    Store it somewhere safe. Never share it in public.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in Verba">
    Open your verb settings and paste the token in the Discord section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite the bot">
    Use the OAuth URL and choose the permissions you need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keep it smooth

- Start in a test server.
- Give the bot only the permissions it needs.
- Watch a few conversations before going live.
